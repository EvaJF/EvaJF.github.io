Hello, I'm Eva Feillet.

Welcome on my homepage!

I am a PhD student on Frugal Incremental Learning at CEA-list / CentraleSupélec MICS in Paris area, France. I am working under the supervision of Dr. Adrian Popescu (CEA-list), Dr. Marina Reyboz (CEA-Leti) and Pr. Céline Hudelot (CentraleSupélec MICS).
I hold an engineering degree from CentraleSupélec Engineering school (M.Sc. in AI).

My previous work experiences include data science internships at IBM Research & Development in Germany and CA-CIB data lab in Singapore, and a research internship on Federated Learning at CEA list in France.

My current reasearch interests include :
- Continual learning, with focus on class-incremental learning
- Computer Vision
- Resource-efficient training of deep neural networks
- Learnig from multi-modal data
- Learning from few data
